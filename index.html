About me

Entrepreneur first,
developer and marketer by craft.

I'm an e-commerce entrepreneur and freelance web developer from Wedel, just outside Hamburg. With my company FLXUniversal I've been running my own private-label brands on Amazon since 2022. I handle everything myself: finding the right product, developing it with manufacturers, getting it shipped to Germany, checking quality, writing the listings and running the PPC campaigns. A few of those brands I've built up and later sold.

On top of that, I've been freelancing since 2020. I build websites and online shops for clients, mostly on Shopify and WooCommerce, and take care of their marketing too: Google, Meta and Microsoft Ads, SEO, analytics and tracking. Product photos, business cards or a restaurant menu? Done those as well.

Right now I'm finishing my B.Sc. in E-Commerce at FH Wedel (done in September 2026). It's a handy combination: what I study during the week is exactly what I already do in practice.
