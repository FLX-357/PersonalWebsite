About me

Entrepreneur first,
developer & marketer by craft.

I'm an e-commerce entrepreneur and freelance web developer from Wedel, near Hamburg. Since 2022 I've built and run my own private-label brands on Amazon — covering the entire journey from product research and development, supplier sourcing and negotiation, logistics to Germany and quality management, through to listing optimization and Amazon PPC. Several of my brands I've successfully built up and sold.

Alongside that, I've worked as a freelancer since 2020, creating websites and online shops for clients — mainly on Shopify and WooCommerce — and running their marketing: Google, Meta and Microsoft Ads, SEO, analytics and tracking. I also handle product photography and design work, from business cards to restaurant menus.

I'm currently completing my B.Sc. in E-Commerce at FH Wedel (graduating September 2026), where theory meets what I practice every day: online retail, web development, marketing, analytics and e-commerce law.
