Insights

What my thesis dug into.

My bachelor thesis in one sentence: what happens to credibility when marketing content is labeled as AI-generated?

The question

AI-generated content is everywhere in marketing. But how does openly labeling it change the way people judge credibility?

Why it matters

Labeling rules for AI content are getting stricter and audiences are getting more skeptical. Every brand using AI will face this question sooner or later.

My takeaway

Hiding AI won't be an option much longer. Brands that figure out early how to be transparent about it will have the easier job. Happy to talk details.
